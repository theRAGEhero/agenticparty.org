Agentic Party is a static informational website for the Agentic Party initiative.

## Cookies and tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies.

We do not currently use analytics cookies, advertising pixels, embedded social widgets, or third-party tracking scripts. The Telegram link is a normal outbound link to `https://t.me/agenticparty`.

If this changes, this notice should be updated before those tools are enabled.

## Server logs

Like most websites, the server may automatically process technical log data when you visit the site. This can include IP address, date and time of access, requested page, browser user agent, referrer, and basic request status.

These logs are used for security, debugging, abuse prevention, and service reliability. They are not used to profile visitors.

## Contact by email or Telegram

If you contact us by email or through Telegram, we process the information you choose to send, such as your name, contact details, organization, message content, and any follow-up context needed to respond.

We use this information to answer your request, discuss possible workshops or services, and manage the conversation you started.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Data may be processed by infrastructure and communication providers needed to operate the website, email, hosting, logs, or Telegram communication.

## Retention

Contact messages are kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, manage the relationship, or handle related administrative needs.

Server logs are kept for operational and security purposes and should be deleted or rotated when no longer needed.

## Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data.

## Updates

This privacy policy may be updated when the website, communication channels, or operational setup changes.
